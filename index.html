About

I was born and raised in Porto, Portugal, where I got a MSc in Informatics Engineering and Computing at the Faculty of Engineering between 2010 and 2015. I worked in Portugal until 2017, when I moved to Copenhagen, where I am currently based.

I currently work for Apple as a UI developer in the Media Products division.
Before that, I worked for Saxo Bank, also as a frontend developer, and for FCo., a Portuguese company which specializes in multimedia systems for cultural institutions.

I am a warm and social person, who establishes new acquaintances and friendships rapidly. I am passionate about developing high quality, reliable software, automated testing, exploring new tehcnologies and empowering other developers. I also have a weak spot for cooking and cycling.
